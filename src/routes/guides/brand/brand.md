## Identity

**Doxa** (δόξα) means glory, radiance, splendor.

Pronunciation: **DOK-sah** (rhymes with moxa).

Primary display name: **Doxa Labs**. GitHub org: **doxalabs**.

## Brand Voice

- Write like a senior dev talking to a peer.
- Be warm, not corporate.
- Show the work instead of boasting.
- Use short sentences and simple words.

## Core Palette

- Clay: `#B08968`
- Umber: `#3E2C20`
- Sand: `#DDB892`
- Linen: `#F5EDE4`
- Parchment: `#FDFAF6`

Rule: keep neutrals warm; avoid pure black and sterile grays.

## Typography

- Primary sans: DM Sans (self-hosted).
- Code font: JetBrains Mono (or equivalent self-hosted mono).
- Favor semibold headings and readable body text over decorative display styles.
- Maintain comfortable reading width around `65ch` for long-form content.

## Design Principles

- Let content breathe with generous spacing.
- Warm, not sterile: soft radii and warm-tinted shadows.
- Quiet confidence: subtle motion, no gratuitous animation.
- Craft in details: consistent icons, states, and micro-interactions.
